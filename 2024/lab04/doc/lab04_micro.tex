Finding $k_n$:

\[
  k_n = k_n'\Big( \frac{W}{L} \Big)
\]

\[
  V_{OV} = V_{GS} - V_t
\]

\[
  i_D = \frac{1}{2} k_n'\Big( \frac{W}{L} \Big) V_{OV}^2
\]

\[
  i_D = \frac{1}{2} k_n (V_{GS} - V_t)^2
\]

\begin{equation}
  k_n = \frac{2 i_D}{(V_{GS} - V_t)^2}
  \label{eq:kn}
\end{equation}
